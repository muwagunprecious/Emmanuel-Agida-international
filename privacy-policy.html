Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Important to Us

Last Updated: 31st December 2025

Emmanuel Agida International ("EAI," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and security of personal information entrusted to us by individuals, organizations, partners, and stakeholders who engage with our services, programs, platforms, and websites.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with EAI and its affiliated entities, including the Emmanuel Agida Foundation.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

a. Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Organization or institution
  • Job title or professional background
  • Location (country, state, city)

b. Professional & Organizational Information

  • Corporate or institutional details
  • Program participation data
  • Event registration information
  • Consultancy engagement details

c. Technical & Usage Data

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Website interaction data (via cookies and analytics tools)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Deliver consultancy services, leadership programs, and events
  • Manage registrations, communications, and partnerships
  • Improve our services, programs, and digital platforms
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Share relevant updates, insights, and opportunities (with consent)

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

EAI does not sell or trade personal data. Information may be shared only:

  • With trusted service providers assisting in operations
  • With partners for jointly delivered programs (where applicable)
  • Where required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • With your explicit consent

All third parties are required to maintain confidentiality and data protection standards.

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement appropriate technical, organizational, and administrative measures to safeguard personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

However, no digital system is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or updates
  • Withdraw consent for communications
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)

Requests can be made via our official contact channels.

6. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience, analyze traffic, and improve functionality. You may control cookie settings through your browser.

7. Third-Party Links

Our platforms may contain links to third-party websites. EAI is not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

8. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected on our website with a revised "Last Updated" date.
