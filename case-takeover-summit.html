Take Over Leadership Development Summit 2025

Take Over Summit Youth & Leadership October 30, 2025
Leadership Development Youth Engagement Capacity Development

Project Overview

The Take Over Leadership Development Summit 2025 was designed as a high-impact leadership development symposium focused on equipping young and emerging leaders with the mindset, skills, and civic awareness required to drive sustainable change in governance, business, and society.

Emmanuel Agida International (EAI) executed the end-to-end project management for the summit, ensuring strategic coordination, seamless delivery, and alignment with the summit's leadership development objectives.

Held on October 30, 2025, the summit convened young leaders, professionals, and change-makers from diverse backgrounds, creating a platform for dialogue, inspiration, and leadership capacity building.

Objectives

The summit sought to:

  • Inspire young people to take responsibility for leadership and nation-building
  • Provide access to experienced public and private sector leaders
  • Promote values-driven leadership, civic engagement, and personal development
  • Foster cross-sector conversations on leadership, governance, and service

EAI's Role & Scope of Work

Emmanuel Agida International provided full-scale project management, covering:

  • Strategic planning and program coordination
  • Speaker engagement and stakeholder liaison
  • Event logistics and operational execution
  • Program flow and session management
  • On-ground coordination and delivery oversight

EAI's approach ensured that the summit was delivered with professionalism, clarity of purpose, and strong participant engagement.

Program Highlights & Speakers

The summit featured high-profile speakers and thought leaders who shared practical insights drawn from public service, corporate leadership, and the creative industry.

Notable Speakers:

Hon. Adewumi Adeyemi
Member, Osun State House of Assembly
Mr. Oluwadamilola Emmanuel
General Manager, Lagos State Water Authority and Special Adviser to the Lagos State Government
Ms. Omotunde Adebowale
Actress and Multi-Award-Winning Television Presenter

Sessions focused on leadership responsibility, public service, career development, influence, and the role of young people in shaping Nigeria's future.

Impact & Outcomes

The Take Over Leadership Development Summit:

  • Engaged a broad network of young emerging leaders
  • Strengthened leadership awareness and civic consciousness among participants
  • Provided direct mentorship exposure through interaction with experienced leaders
  • Reinforced the importance of ethical, service-oriented leadership

The summit contributed to building a pipeline of informed, motivated young leaders prepared to take active roles in their communities and professional spheres.

Value Delivered by Emmanuel Agida International

Through disciplined project management and strategic coordination, EAI ensured:

  • Timely and effective execution of a multi-speaker leadership symposium
  • Positive stakeholder experience for speakers, partners, and participants
  • A well-structured program aligned with leadership development goals

The engagement demonstrates EAI's capacity to design, manage, and deliver leadership-focused platforms that translate vision into measurable social impact.
