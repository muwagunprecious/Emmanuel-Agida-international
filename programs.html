Programs Arm

Building Capacity, Fostering Innovation

Overview

The Programs Arm develops and implements multi-sectoral programs designed to build capacity, foster innovation, and enhance socio-economic development.

This arm serves as the engine of implementation for EAI’s thematic initiatives, focusing on key sectors that directly influence leadership growth, youth empowerment, and national development.

Key Areas of Focus

  • Policy Development and Governance: Promoting sound leadership, accountability, and civic responsibility.
  • Personal and Youth Development: Building competencies, ethics, and purpose-driven mindsets among emerging leaders.
  • Technology and Innovation: Driving inclusion and advancement through digital literacy, AI integration, and tech entrepreneurship.
  • Finance and Economic Empowerment: Promoting financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and inclusive economic participation.
  • Education and Human Capital Development: Supporting access to transformative education and knowledge-based growth.

Programs & Initiatives

The Programs Arm also coordinates EAI’s flagship events and learning initiatives. Programs will be implemented through cross-sector partnerships with key public and private institutions.

  • The Achievers Summit (In commemoration of the international youths day and Emmanuel Agida’s Birthday)
  • 100Under40 Awards in Leadership and Entrepreneurship (An International Youth Day Event)
  • Youth Leadership Development Masterclass
  • Policy Dialogues and Civic Engagement Series
  • Women in Leadership and Governance Initiative
