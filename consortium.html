The Local Consortium

A Structured Leadership & Civic Engagement Network

Overview

The Local Consortium is the structural representation of EAI's leadership and development network across colleges, states, and national levels. It provides a formal, democratic, and values-driven platform for leadership incubation, peer-to-peer learning, civic participation, and institutional governance.

Purpose of the Consortium

  • Develop ethical, responsible, and globally competent leaders.
  • Institutionalize leadership learning and governance practice.
  • Foster civic responsibility, patriotism, and peacebuilding.
  • Serve as a pipeline from grassroots leadership to national influence.

Consortium Levels

Three interconnected tiers that move leaders from campus to country.

Collegiate Consortium

  • Campus-based leadership chapters.
  • Practical leadership labs, peer learning, and community projects.
  • Launchpad for responsible campus governance and social impact.

State Consortium

  • Coordinates and expands leadership practice across states.
  • Connects collegiate chapters into a statewide civic network.
  • Drives collaboration with state institutions, partners, and communities.

National Consortium

  • Sets national strategy, policy engagement, and partnerships.
  • Bridges grassroots leadership to national influence.
  • Champions a unified agenda for ethical governance and peacebuilding.
